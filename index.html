Mineral Water

  • Mineral water is water sourced from natural springs containing various minerals.
  • It typically includes elements like calcium, magnesium, and potassium.
  • Unlike tap water, it’s often bottled at the source and not chemically treated.
  • People drink mineral water for both hydration and potential health benefits.
  • It can be still or naturally carbonated, depending on the source.
